
>> New Version checklist <<

**
** TCTRAFFIC
**

1. Up the versionname and versioncode in the manifest.
	- the versioncode is used in the trafficmap URL path 
	  (ie ".../mobilecontent/tctraffic" + versioncode + "/trafficmap.php")
2. cp -rp  ".../mobilecontent/tctraffic" + (versioncode - 1) to ".../mobilecontent/tctraffic" + versioncode 
	- a directory matching the versioncode must exist and will be automatically used.
